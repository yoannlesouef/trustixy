## How It Works

**Three steps:**

1. **Invite your client** — they install the SDK and their AI agents are catalogued automatically. No manual forms.

2. **They request your review** — when ready, the client submits a co-signature request. You receive a notification with the full document, risk classification, and SDK evidence trail.

3. **You review and sign** — 10–20 minutes per system. Your name, firm, and date are permanently embedded. The client downloads a professionally endorsed compliance PDF.

## What You're Co-signing

Co-signature confirms that you have:

1. Reviewed the AI system description and use case
2. Verified the risk classification is appropriate to your professional judgment
3. Validated that the listed obligations are applicable and complete
4. Applied your expertise to the overall compliance assessment

**It is not a legal guarantee of full regulatory compliance.** The scope is defined precisely in the partner agreement. The client remains responsible for implementing the obligations.

**Before applying**, we recommend:
- Confirming with your professional indemnity insurer that reviewing AI compliance documents falls within your covered practice
- Reviewing your professional association's position on AI-assisted compliance work

Most accounting and legal professional bodies in France, Belgium, and the Netherlands have issued guidance covering this type of advisory work.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does a review take?**
10–20 minutes for a well-documented system. The questionnaire is pre-filled from SDK behavioral data — you review and confirm rather than starting from a blank form. Complex high-risk systems may take longer.

**Do I need technical AI expertise?**
No. The classification is produced by Trustixy's AI and pre-filled from operational data. Your role is professional judgment — is the risk level appropriate? Are the obligations applicable? The same judgment you apply to any compliance assessment.

**Can I co-sign for clients not linked to my account?**
No. Co-signature is only available for clients you have invited or who joined via your partner link. This ensures you have an existing client relationship before reviewing their systems.

**What happens if I disagree with the AI's classification?**
You request changes and explain why. The client updates the description and re-submits. Your professional judgment always takes precedence over the AI output.

**Can multiple people at my firm co-sign?**
Yes. Add team members from Settings → Team. Each person's name and role appears on the documents they co-sign.

**Is there a minimum volume commitment?**
No. Per-act model has no minimums. Integrator license is month-to-month.
