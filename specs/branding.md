# Branding – Trustixy

## Name

Trustixy

## Positioning

AI Action Audit Platform — for developers, security teams, and compliance officers.

## Colors

* Primary: #0F172A (dark blue)
* Accent: #3B82F6 (blue)
* Success: #10B981 (green — compliant status, obligations complete)
* Warning: #F59E0B (amber — needs review, sensitive file alert)
* Danger: #EF4444 (red — unacceptable risk, critical alert)
* Background: #F9FAFB

## Tone

* Technical but accessible
* Direct — no compliance jargon unless necessary
* Trustworthy — precision language, no marketing fluff
* Developer-friendly — show code, not buzzwords

## Taglines

* "Every AI action, fully traced." — primary product tagline
* "Audit logs for AI agents." — developer-facing
* "Your AI compliance, backed by real data." — compliance-facing
* "The compliance platform your clients can sign." — partner-facing (highlights co-signature)
