# Trustixy – Product Definition

## Tagline

AI Trust & Compliance, by design.

## Core Promise

Stay compliant with the EU AI Act — structured, traceable, and human-validated.

Trustixy is not a document generator. It is a **living compliance registry**: a structured, auditable record of every AI system your organization uses, how each was assessed, under which regulatory version, what changed over time — and validated by a qualified expert when it matters.

## Value Proposition

Any LLM can generate a compliance document. What Trustixy provides that no prompt can replace:

- A **versioned registry** of all AI systems, never a one-time snapshot
- A **full classification history** linked to the regulatory version in effect at assessment time
- **Automatic re-assessment triggers** when the EU AI Act is updated
- An **actionable obligations checklist** per system — not just a document, but tasks to complete
- **Prescriber co-signature** on compliance documents — turning an AI-generated output into a professionally endorsed deliverable
- An **explicit legal disclaimer** on every document — protecting Trustixy and users from false compliance claims
- A **structured evidence trail** — timestamped, versioned, audit-ready

## Legal Scope & Disclaimer Policy

Trustixy generates compliance documentation using AI. Every document produced by the platform carries a mandatory disclaimer:

> *"This document was generated by Trustixy using AI assistance. It is provided to support your compliance process and does not constitute legal advice. Trustixy cannot be held liable for regulatory decisions made solely on the basis of this document. For legally binding compliance, we recommend co-signature by a qualified prescriber partner or review by a qualified legal professional."*

This disclaimer is versioned (`disclaimer_version`) and stored with every document. It is non-removable unless a qualified prescriber co-signs the document, in which case the prescriber assumes professional responsibility for their review.

## Human Validation Flow

Trustixy integrates a lightweight human validation layer to bridge the gap between AI-generated assessments and legally defensible compliance:

1. **Confidence scoring** — every classification includes a confidence score. Low-confidence classifications (< 70%) are flagged and prompt the user to request expert review.
2. **Expert review request** — users can request review from their prescriber partner directly from the system page.
3. **Prescriber co-signature** — the prescriber reviews the classification and document in the partner portal, then approves or requests changes. On approval, their name, firm, and date are embedded in the document.
4. **Co-signed status** — co-signed documents display a verified badge and carry stronger evidential weight than unsigned ones.

## Revenue Models

Trois modèles économiques, tous prescripteur-led. Trustixy est l'infrastructure ; le prescripteur possède la relation client.

### Modèle 1 — Co-signature per acte (cabinets, consultants)
Le prescripteur fixe le prix de chaque co-signature (€150–500). Trustixy prélève €25 par acte via Stripe Connect. Le prescripteur encaisse directement. Économie : 20 clients × 2 co-signatures × €300 = €12 000/an pour le prescripteur, €1 000/an pour Trustixy — mais scalable avec le réseau.

### Modèle 2 — Licence fédération
Contrat annuel unique avec une fédération professionnelle : €3 000–10 000/an selon le nombre de membres. La fédération distribue à ses membres (accès Pro automatique), Trustixy ne gère qu'un seul interlocuteur. Objectif : 5 fédérations an 1 = €25 000–40 000 ARR.

### Modèle 3 — Licence intégrateur
Abonnement €299–599/mois pour les ESN/SSII. La conformité devient une annexe standard du livrable projet (facturée au client). API incluse, white-label inclus. Objectif : 10 intégrateurs an 1 = €36 000–72 000 ARR.

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1 — Prescriber-led growth (B2B2B)
Activer les trois modèles en parallèle : cabinets comptables (Model 1), fédérations numériques (Model 2), intégrateurs AI (Model 3). Les prescripteurs font le commercial, Trustixy fournit l'outil et l'infrastructure de facturation.

### Phase 2 — Direct SME acquisition
Une fois validé via les prescripteurs, ouvrir un funnel freemium self-serve. Documents sans signature disponibles sur les plans Free/Pro ; documents co-signés nécessitent un partenaire prescripteur.

### Phase 3 — Platform & marketplace
Listings marketplace cloud (AWS, Azure), API embarquée pour les intégrateurs, expansion multi-réglementations (GDPR, NIS2, AI Liability Directive).
