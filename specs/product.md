# Trustixy – Product Definition

## Tagline

AI Trust & Compliance, simplified

## Core Promise

Get compliant with the EU AI Act in minutes.

## Target Users

### End Users (who use the platform)
* SMEs using AI tools
* CTO / Product teams
* Legal / compliance teams

### Prescribers (who recommend and distribute Trustixy)
* Accounting & audit firms — advise SMEs on regulatory obligations
* Law firms & legal consultants — deliver AI compliance mandates
* IT service companies & integrators — bundle compliance into AI project deliverables
* Industry federations & trade associations — provide tools to their SME members
* Cloud & AI vendors — offer compliance as a marketplace add-on

## Core Features

1. AI Systems Registry
2. Risk Classification
3. Compliance Document Generator
4. Audit Log
5. Export (PDF)

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1 — Prescriber-led growth (B2B2B)
Target accounting firms and IT integrators first. They are already in front of SMEs, already billing for compliance advisory, and looking for tools to productize their advice. A pilot with 2-3 partners validates the channel faster than any direct marketing campaign.

### Phase 2 — Direct SME acquisition
Once brand and product are validated through prescribers, open a self-serve freemium funnel to capture inbound demand from the EU AI Act regulatory pressure.

### Phase 3 — Platform & marketplace
List on cloud marketplaces (AWS, Azure, Google) and open an API for integrators to embed compliance features in their own products.

## V1 Requirements

* Landing page
* Signup / Login
* Dashboard
* System detail page
* Document generation
